About Us

Our Mission

The Pet & Vet veterinary clinic in Niterói was created to realize our dream of creating a place of caring for pets and providing veterinary medicine in a single location full of love and affection for our patients - the animals.

We believe that practicing veterinary medicine with attentiveness and passion enhances the treatment and healing aspects for our patients.

We consider our patients to be not only companion animals - but also our children, our brothers and sisters, true members of the family.
